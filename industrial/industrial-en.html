High-performance instant cyanoacrylate adhesives for industry, maintenance and also for domestic use.

Cuantum is a company founded in 2015 with a robust background in the field of adhesives, particularly in the realm of cyanoacrylate instant adhesives.

Cuantum's objective is to deliver cutting-edge solutions, providing products of added value and high performance. To achieve this goal, we are equipped with proprietary technology development projects, application laboratories, research and development facilities, and domestic production capabilities.

Cuantum has a wide variety of cyanoacrylate adhesives in various formats adapted to each application:

  • Ethyl cyanoacrylate
  • Methoxyethyl cyanoacrylate
  • Butyl cyanoacrylate
  • Octyl cyanoacrylate
  • CROSSTEC© Technology
  • Light curable
  • Two components (2K)
  • Activators

    Cuantum E 100 PLUS and Cuantum E1000 PLUS adhesives with patented CROSSTEC© technology provide greater chemical, thermal and humidity resistance.

    New Cuantum INSTANT REPAIR on command adhesive with thixotropic gel consistency, double curing mechanism with blue light and humidity. Its easy machining (sanding, drilling and painting) makes it ideal for repairing plastic and wooden parts.

    Light Cure Cuantum LC: Dual cure, flash cure on demand for irradiated areas and moisture cure for non-irradiated areas.

    Cuantum CROSSTEC©: Formulations doped with small amounts of crosslinking monomers that form crosslinked polymers: more stable to water, temperature and chemical products.

    New product QUANTUM INSTANT REPAIR on command! A light-curable and machinable adhesive, ideal for repairs.
